# Suggested tiers

ASO Basic
- Price: Free
- Suggested limit: 10k verifications/month
- More tolerant thresholds

ASOShieldPro
- Price: R$ 79/month
- Suggested limit: 100k verifications/month
- Stronger thresholds and step-up

ASOShieldEnterprise
- Price: starts at R$ 699/month
- Suggested limit: custom
- Per-route policies and stronger decisions
